About Us

Colors of Hope Foundation for Good Health is a faith-based non-governmental organization (NGO) founded on the 8th of february 2013 by Dr. Enefo Emoruwa. We are dedicated to raising a generation of children living in Lagos State's most challenging slum communities, including Sogunro, Makoko, and Oko Agbon, who are healthy, God fearing and educated. Our mission is rooted in the belief that every child, regardless of their circumstances, deserves access to quality education, healthcare and biblical discipleship.


We are inspired/instructed by Mark 16:15, which calls us to "Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all creation." For us, this means not only sharing the message of faith but also extending a helping hand to those in need. We firmly believe that through education and healthcare, we can fulfill this mission by improving the lives of the most vulnerable among us.

Our Mission
Our primary purpose is to provide free medical and educational care for children in the slum community. We are driven by our mission to embody Christ's love and teachings, empowering lives and sharing the message of salvation through our actions and outreach.

Our Programs And Projects

At Colors of Hope Foundation, we offer a range of programs and services designed to make a lasting impact on the lives of children in slum communities. Our medical initiatives include free health check-ups and treatments, while our educational programs encompass tutoring and mentoring.

Target Community

The Sogunro slum community in Lagos state Nigeria, where I work, is plagued with many vices. It is densely populated, lacks standard hospitals and schools, has a very high birthrate and high rate of teenage pregnancy. The women give birth to children with no good and adequate plan for care. This has to lead to a very high rate of sick and out of school children. In the next 5-10 years, I want to successfully reduce child mortality to the barest minimum, improve on the women's reproductive health and ensure that more children in the community go to school.
